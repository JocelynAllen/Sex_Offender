How to Stay Safe

Personal Alarms

A personal alarm is an inexpensive self-defense device. When activated,the alarm will emit a very loud alarm siren. The main purpose of a personal alarm is to scare the attacker away.

Safety Plans

Have a plan to get out of uncomfortable situations. Let others know where you are going. Visualize your escape plan and how you would get away.
